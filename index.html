    INTRODUCTION

    Calgary's HomeSick has carved out a lane for himself in the world of high-BPM club music — not just by running the Footwork Jungle online community and the Percolate party series, but also through his own, oftentimes amorphous productions and world-class DJ sets.

    WORLD TOUR 2019

    HomeSick is currently fielding dates for a tour with across Europe, the UK and Canada, in support of his upcoming release with German record label Defrostatica. Get in contact to bring HomeSick to your city.

    RBMA ALUMNI

    October 2015 HomeSick was selected to be a participant at the Red Bull Music Academy hosted in Paris, France, where he was mentored by some of the world’s most prestigious artists and producers in the dance music realm.
